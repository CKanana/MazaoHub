Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mazao Hub?

Mazao Hub is an online marketplace that connects local farmers directly with consumers, allowing farmers to sell their fresh produce without the need for middlemen.

How can I become a seller on Mazao Hub?

To become a seller on Mazao Hub, you'll need to create an account and provide details about your farm and the products you offer. Our team will review your application and verify your information before approving you as a seller.

What types of products can I find on Mazao Hub?

Mazao Hub offers a wide variety of fresh produce, including fruits, vegetables, herbs, and other agricultural products. All products are sourced directly from local farms and are guaranteed to be fresh and high-quality.

How can I be sure that the products are fresh and locally sourced?

We take great care in verifying the authenticity and quality of all products listed on our platform. Each seller must provide detailed information about their farm and growing practices, and we conduct regular checks to ensure transparency and accountability.

How do I place an order on Mazao Hub?

To place an order, simply browse our selection of products, add the items you want to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll be able to choose your preferred delivery or pickup option during the checkout process.
